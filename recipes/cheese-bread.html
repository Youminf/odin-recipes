Brazilian Cheese Bread

What is Cheese Bread?

Cheese Bread is a beloved brazilian snack, it has a crunchy outside that hides a delicious, soft and chewy cheesy inside!

Ingredients

  • ½ cup olive oil or butter
  • ⅓ cup water
  • ⅓ cup milk or soy milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups tapioca flour
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • ⅔ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 beaten eggs
